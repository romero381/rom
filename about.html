My Career Journey

The Spark of Creation

My journey began with a fascination for how websites worked. The challenge was turning static ideas into interactive realities. This led me to dive deep into HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, creating my first simple but innovative interactive components.

Embracing Modern Frameworks

As my skills grew, I faced the challenge of building larger, more complex applications. I embraced modern frameworks like React, which empowered me to create scalable, data-driven UIs and innovative single-page applications.

Mastering Responsive & Accessible Design

A major focus has been ensuring my creations are usable by everyone, on any device. The challenge of supporting countless screen sizes and user needs pushed me to master responsive design principles and accessibility standards, making the web a more inclusive place.

Looking to the Future

The web is always evolving, and so am I. My goal is to continue pushing the boundaries of front-end development, exploring new technologies like WebGL and WebAssembly to create next-generation web experiences that are not just websites, but true interactive art.
